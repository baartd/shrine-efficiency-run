Cemetery of Ash

  • Grab Ashen Estus Flask (In front of the fountain)
  • Kill Iudex Gundyr

Firelink Shrine

  • OPTIONAL Kill Sword Master Saber (Left of Firelink Shrine)
  • Get Collapse Gesture (Speak to Hawkwood the Deserter)
  • Get Hurrah! Gesture (Speak to Blacksmith Andre)
  • OPTIONAL Allot Estus

High Wall of Lothric

  • Grab Cell Key (Activate Tower on the Wall Bonfire on the way)
  • Grab Estus Shard (Same room as Cell Key)
  • Recruit Greirat of the Undead Settlement
  • Get Small Lothric Banner (Speak to Emma)
  • Get Way of Blue Covenant (Speak to Emma again)
  • Kill Vordt of the Boreal Valley (You can summon Sword Master Saber & Lion Knight Albert)

Undead Settlement

  • Recruit Yoel of Londor
  • Grab Loretta's Bone (It's on a hanging corpse around the large bonfire)
  • Open shortcut door (To the right of the large bonfire)
  • Grab Estus Shard (In front of the large bonfire)
  • Grab Warrior of Sunlight Covenant (In building behind large bonfire)
  • Grab Undead Bone Shard (In area that the Giant Archer shoots at)
  • Grab Mortician's Ashes (In area that the Giant Archer shoots at)
  • Recruit Cornyx of the Great Swamp (Activate Cliff Underside Bonfire on the way)

Road of Sacrifices

  • Grab Braille Divine Tome of Carim (Drop down to lower area under bridge)
  • Get Blue Sentinels Covenant (Speak to Horace the Hushed)
  • Exhaust Anri's Dialogue
  • Grab Estus Shard (Behind broken down Chapel & activate Crucifixion Woods Bonfire on the way)
  • OPTIONAL: Recruit Orbeck of Vinheim (Located at top of the building before Crystal Sage) Requres 10 int or wait until getting a ring that boosts Intelligence
  • SIRRIS / HEYSEL QUEST REWARD DILEMMA : You need to kill Yellowfinger Heysel when you're embered if you want the Proper Bow Gesture. But if you want Sirris' questline to continue, you can ignore Heysel
  • Grab Farron Coal (Heavy, Sharp & Poison Infusions) that is guarded by a Black Knight inside a ruined keep
  • Grab Great Swamp Pyromancy Tome (At the base of a tree to the right of the Exile Knights)

Road of Sacrifices - Farron Keep

  • Grab Estus Shard (Next to a broken down tower that can be seen from the Farron Keep Bonfire)
  • Extinguist 1st Flame Tower (Follow the swamp fires to find Flame Towers)
  • Grab Sage's Coal (Crystal, Blessed & Deep Infusions) from behind the First Flame Tower to the left
  • Extinguist 2nd Flame Tower
  • Grab Sage's Scroll (Take sharp left from Keep Ruins Bonfire when facing the Gate with Three Flames)
  • Grab Undead Bone Shard (Inside tower filled with Slugs to the right of Keep Ruins Bonfire)
  • Grab Dreamchaser's Ashes (Behind illusory wall near Old Wolf of Farron)
  • Get Legion Etiquette Gesture (Interact with Old Wolf of Farron)
  • Get Watchdogs of Farron Covenant (Speak to Old Wolf of Farron again)
  • Extinguish Final Flame Tower (Straight ahead down the ladder from Old Wolf of Farron)
  • Kill Yellowfinger Heysel in case you missed her the first time (She will invade near where you find Golden Scroll if you're embered)
  • Grab Golden Scroll (Behind the Final Flame Tower in a cave guarded by Basilisks)

Firelink Shrine pit stop 1

  • Give both Ashes to Shrine Handmaid
  • Purchase Grave Key (1500 Souls)
  • Give the Scrolls to Orbeck (If you've recruited him already)
  • Upgrade Estus Flask & Bonfire intensity

Undead Settlement - Cliff Underside Bonfire

  • Get Mound-makers Covenant (Speak to Holy Knight Hodrick after stepping into the cage on the back of a large non-hostile saw wielding creature)
  • Travel to last Bonfire using a Homeward Bone (You can get a Homeward Bone by exhausting Hodrick's dialogue)
  • Get Prayer Gesture (Recruit Irina of Carim)
  • OPTIONAL Speak to Eygon of Carim for access to his summon sign at later bosses
  • Get Toast Gesture (Speak to Siegward after killing Fire Demon together)
  • Get Sleep Gesture (Speak to Siegward again)
  • Grab Pale Tongue from a hanging corpse near the large Bonfire (You can break some caskets and walk up onto the roof of the small house to reach the corpse)
  • Kill Curse-Rotted Greatwood

Road of Sacrifices - Crucifixion Woods Bonfire

  • Kill Crystal Sage (You can summon Eygon in the room below Orbeck if you're not wearing an auto-summon Covenant such as Way of Blue)

Cathedral of the Deep

  • Grab Paladin's Ashes (Guarded by Fallen Knight left of first Bonfire)
  • Grab Estus Shard (Outside Cleansing Chapel)
  • Grab Undead Bone Shard (In area that the Giant Archer shoots at)
  • Get Deep Braille Divine Tome (Kill or pacify the Mimic past the room with two Giants)
  • Kill Longfinger Kirk for his drops (You need to be embered for him to invade)
  • OPTIONAL Grab Pale Tongue in the deep water (Just behind the giant closest to the Deacon's of the Deep room)
  • Take Elevator up and open the large doors (They lead outside to the area the Giant Archer shoots at)
  • Get tricked by Unbreakable Patches disguised as Siegward (By walking over the raised bridge)
  • Get Rosaria's Fingers Covenant by speaking to Rosaria, Mother of Rebirth (And open 2nd shortcut to Cleansing Chapel Bonfire on the way)
  • OPTIONAL Grab Pale Tongue from the end of the path leading to the rafters that you need to cross to get to Rosaria's Bedchamber
  • Get Red Sign Soapstone (Kill the non-hostile worm-creature)
  • Get Prostration Gesture (Speak to Patches)
  • Purchase all 4 pieces of the Catarina set from Patches (15000 Souls in total and you could purchase these after killing the Deacons of the Deep)
  • Get Rejoice Gesture (Speak to Siegward calling you from inside the well near the Cleansing Chapel Bonfire)
  • Kill Deacons of the Deep (You can summon Anri & Horace for this boss fight)

Firelink Shrine pit stop 2

  • Draw out true strength at Yoel five times (And in between you let your character fall to it's death / further the questlines below)
  • Give Paladin's Ashes to Shrine Handmaid
  • OPTIONAL Purchase Dried Finger (2000 Souls)
  • Exhaust Sirris' dialogue (Get Darkmoon Loyalty Gesture)
  • SIRRIS / HEYSEL QUEST REWARD DILEMMA : Kill Sirris if you plan to get the Proper Bow Gesture
  • Speak to Anri
  • Give Transposing Kiln to Ludleth of Courland
  • Get 5 Cracked Red-Eye Orbs (Speak to Ringfinger Leonhard)
  • Exhaust Hawkwood's dialogue
  • Get Beckon Gesture (Speak to Yoel)
  • Give Loretta's Bone to Greirat
  • Give Coals to Andre
  • Upgrade Estus Flask
  • Get Pyromancy Flame (Speak to Cornyx)
  • Give Pyromancy Tome to Cornyx
  • Get Welcome Gesture (Speak to Cornyx again)
  • Give Braille Divine Tome of Carim to Irina (Not the Deep Tome)
  • Get Lift Chamber Key (Speak to Leonhard again)
  • Get Curl up Gesture (Interact with Greirat while he's curled up)
  • Send Greirat away to scavenge
  • Get Dignified Bow Gesture (Speak to Yuria of Londor)
  • Purchase Londor Braille Divine Tome from Yuria (50 Souls)
  • Anger Yuria by hitting her a few times (This opens up the possiblity to get the rewards from both the ally & enemy questlines in one playthrough)
  • Upgrade Bonfire intensity

Cathedral of the Deep (KILLED SIRRIS)

  • SIRRIS / HEYSEL QUEST REWARD DILEMMA : Give a Pale Tongue to Rosaria (You'll forfeit Sirris' questline) or don't give her anything (You'll forfeit the Proper Bow Gesture)

Road of Sacrifices - Farron Keep Bonfire

  • Kill Londor Pale Shade (Hug the wall to the right, near the bonfire, and he'll invade if you're embered)
  • Make use of Black Separation Crystal and / or Dried Finger to get the NPC Summon Gestures before fighting the Abyss Watchers
  • SIRRIS / HEYSEL QUEST REWARD DILEMMA : Get Proper Bow Gesture (Summon Yellowfinger Heysel just outside the tower near the Keep Ruins Bonfire) if you've followed the questline that caused Sirris' questline to fail
  • Get By My Sword Gesture (Summon Black Hand Gotthard located on the pathway in front of the Abyss Watchers Gate)
  • Kill Abyss Watchers

Catacombs of Carthus

  • Grab Carthus Pyromancy Tome (Drop down on the platform on the left about halfway across the bridge)
  • Speak to Anri (Located in an alcove at the top of the Boulder Bridge)
  • Grab Undead Bone Shard (Kill skeleton wearing a hat at bottom of Boulder Bridge and wait for Skeletal Boulder to crash into the gate)
  • Grab Grave Warden's Ashes (Follow the boulder down from the Catacombs of Carthus Bonfire and it'll be up the stairs on the far left)
  • OPTIONAL Kill Knight Slayer Tsorig for his summon sign at Old Demon King & the My thanks! Gesture. Or you can kill him in Smouldering Lake.

Smouldering Lake

  • Kill Horace (Take a sharp right into a cave just as you step into the water in Smouldering Lake)
  • Get Undead Bone Shard (Kill Carthus Sandworm)
  • Get Undead Bone Shard (Far left of the long room just after taking the stairs down in the first room after the Demon Ruins Bonfire)
  • Grab Izalith Pyromancy Tome (Located in middle of room adjacent to Old King's Antechamber Bonfire)
  • Grab Estus Shard (Behind illusory wall near Old King's Antechamber Bonfire)
  • Grab Quelana Pyromancy Tome (In the room hidden behind an illusory wall, the room above the Giant Rat)
  • OPTIONAL IF YOU'VE KILLED KNIGHT SLAYER TSORIG EARLIER : Get My Thanks! Gesture (Kill Knight Slayer Tsorig just past the Basilisk room)
  • OPTIONAL Summon Great Swamp Cuculus and make sure she survives when you kill the Old Demon King (This way Cornyx' Set will be obtainable in the Undead Settlement)

Catacombs of Carthus - Catacombs of Carthus Bonfire

  • Get Ring of the Evil Eye (Speak to Anri)
  • Grab Grave Warden Pyromancy Tome in Wolnir's Boss room (And kill Wolnir)

Irithyll of the Boreal Valley

  • SIRRIS / HEYSEL QUEST REWARD DILEMMA : Help Sirris defend against Creighton the Wanderer (Walk back to where the Sulyvahn Beast ambushed you and interact with her summon sign) if you chose her questline over Heysel's
  • Get Quiet Resolve Gesture (Speak to Anri at the Church of Yorshka Bonfire)
  • Grab Undead Bone Shard (At the large gravestone just past the Church of Yorshka)
  • Kill Creighton who invades near the large gravestone (You need to be embered)
  • Grab Excrement-covered Ashes (And activate Distant Manor Bonfire on the way)
  • Open up the way towards Pontiff Sulyvahn (Do not speak to Siegward until told to do so)
  • Kill Londor Pale Shade (He'll invade when you're embered on the way towards Pontiff Sulyvahn)

Undead Settlement - Absolution

  • Interact with statue of Velka (And Request Absolution)

Firelink Shrine pit stop 3

  • Speak to Eygon
  • SIRRIS / HEYSEL QUEST REWARD DILEMMA : Speak to Sirris if you chose her questline over Heysel's
  • Give Ashes to Shrine Handmaid
  • Exhaust Hawkwood's dialogue to get the Farron Ring (He might be outside Firelink Shrine at a grave to the left)
  • Upgrade Estus Flask
  • Give Carthus & Izalith Pyromancy Tomes to Cornyx
  • Upgrade Bonfire intensity

High Wall of Lothric - Tower on the Wall Bonfire

  • Get Red Eye Orb (Kill the Darkwraith underneath Tower on the Wall Bonfire)
  • Get Basin of Vows (Kill Emma)
  • Kill Dancer of the Boreal Valley (You can summon other players before placing the Basin down behind where Emma used to be)

Consumed King's Garden

  • Grab Estus Shard (Just right of where you jump off the first elevator about halfway on it's way down)
  • Kill Oceiros the Consumed King (Hawkwood's summon sign is located in the middle of the open platform just before the two knights guarding Oceiros)
  • Grab Path of the Dragon Gesture (In front of the fountain)

Untended Graves

  • Kill Champion Gundyr (Sword Master Saber can be summoned for this fight)
  • Grab Coiled Sword Fragment (If already in possession of this item, it's smart to first trade the old one with the crow for a slab before picking this up)
  • Grab Eyes of a Fire Keeper (In the same place as where Irina sits in Firelink Shrine)

Lothric Castle

  • Grab Undead Bone Shard (Located on the ledge under the bridge underneath the twin dragons)
  • Get Praise the Sun Gesture (Opposite Dragonslayer Armour Boss room and to the right is a ladder that leads to the Altar of Sunlight that grants this gesture)
  • Grab Braille Divine Tome of Lothric (Jump down near one of the dragons in the same area where the ladder towards the Altar of Sunlight is)
  • Kill Dragonslayer Armour (You can summon Eygon inside the elevator building that leads to Lothric or Sirris left of the stairs in front of the fog gate)

Firelink Shrine pit stop 4

  • Get Applause Gesture (Speak to Leonhard)
  • Give Tomes to Irina
  • Kill Irina for her Ashes & the Tower Key
  • Give Ashes to Shrine Handmaid
  • Grab Fire Keeper Soul (Use Tower Key)
  • Speak to Patches (And refuse to forgive him if you want a Rusted Gold Coin)
  • Kick down ladder on the roof where the Crow's Nest is
  • OPTIONAL Grab Homeward Bone right of the Ladder if you don't have any
  • Get Call Over Gesture (Leave Homeward Bone in Crow's Nest)
  • Grab Estus Shard (Located up on the rafters of Firelink Shrine)
  • Give Fire Keeper's Soul & Eyes to the Fire Keeper
  • Upgrade Estus Flask
  • Send Greirat away to scavenge again
  • Upgrade Bonfire intensity

Irithyll of the Boreal Valley - Church of Yorshka Bonfire

  • Get Duel Bow Gesture (Summon Londor Pale Shade located left of Gotthard's summon sign that is located opposite of the fog wall to Pontiff Sulyvahn's boss room)
  • Kill Pontiff Sulyvahn (You can summon Anri & Gotthard for this fight as well)
  • Get Aldrich Faithful Covenant (Speak to Archdeacon McDonnell)
  • Grab Easterner's Ashes (Near the Silver Knight Archers)

Irithyll of the Boreal Valley - Anor Londo

  • Grab Giant's Coal (Lightning, Simple, Chaos Infusions) from the corpse of the Giant Blacksmith
  • Grab Estus Shard (Located in a chest in the main hall at ground level)
  • OPTIONAL Kill Aldrich, Devourer of Gods now or later (Depending on whether you want to continue utilizing the Irithyll of the Boreal Valley / Anor Londo PVP hotspot)
  • Get Blade of the Darkmoon Covenant (Take rotating platform back down and walk across an invisible bridge, then speak to Company Captain Yorshka)
  • Exhaust Siegward's dialogue (He's still in the kitchen near the Distant Manor Bonfire)

Irithyll Dungeon

  • Kill Alva, Seeker of the Spurned for his drops (You need to be embered for him to invade)
  • Grab Estus Shard (Kill Mimic in room just past the stairs that provide a view of the Profaned Capital in the distance)
  • Grab Old Cell Key (Located in the right chest at the end of the sewer tunnel)
  • Grab Dragon Torso Stone (Past the room with 2 Giant Rats and past the tunnel on the left)
  • Grab Profaned Coal (Dark, Blood & Hollow Infusions) from the room across the Giant Rats
  • Grab Xanthous Ashes (Located in the room adjacent to the room containing the Profaned Coal)
  • Grab Stretch Out Gesture & Undead Bone Shard (From the corpse just next to the Profaned Capital Bonfire)
  • Get Logan's Scroll (Kill the Court Sorcerer guarding Siegward's Cell)
  • Get Titanite Slab (Speak to Siegward who is trapped in the cell)
  • Grab Jailer's Key Ring (Up the stairs in the area where the Court Sorcerer was)
  • Kill Yhorm the Giant (Siegward will help you as soon as you enter the Boss room)
  • Grab Prisoner Chief's Ashes (Located in a cell near the Irithyll Dungeon Bonfire)
  • Recruit Karla (Locked in room adjacent to the room with the 2 Giant Rats)

Archdragon Peak

  • Kill Ancient Wyvern
  • Grab Dragon Chaser's Ashes (Located left of the stairs leading up to the Twinkling Dragon Torso Stone)
  • Get Twinkling Dragon Torso Stone (Hawkwood can be summoned to guide the way and his summon sign is just outside the Great Belfry Bonfire room)

Lothric Castle - Grand Archives (IF ALDRICH IS ALIVE KILL HIM)

  • Get Crystal Scroll (Kill Crystal Sage)
  • OPTIONAL If you haven't got 10 Intelligence, grab the Scholar Ring (It's located behind a bookcase shortcut that requires pulling a lever and it's located on one end of the wooden bridge, opposite of the elevator shortcut)

Road of Sacrifices (OPTIONAL IF YOU'VE ALREADY RECRUITED ORBECK)

  • Recruit Orbeck of Vinheim (Located at top of the building before Crystal Sage) and use the Scholar Ring to boost your Intelligence to 10+ if needed

Firelink Shrine pit stop 5

  • Give the Ashes to the Shrine Handmaid
  • Get Patches Squat Gesture (Speak to Patches when he's squatting)
  • Exhaust Yuria's dialogue (She will mention that Orbeck might become a threat & also that your wife corpse Anri awaits you)
  • Speak to Greirat (He will speak of being saved by a peculiar Onion Knight)
  • Give the Pyromancy Tomes to Karla
  • Give the Scrolls to Orbeck
  • Get Silent Ally Gesture (Purchase from Orbeck for 18000 Souls in total : Farron Flashsword, Aural Decoy & Pestilent Mercury as well as all of the spells cheaper than 3000 Souls)
  • OPTIONAL Purchase the rest of Orbecks spells (85000 more Souls) and exhaust his dialogue so he can be summoned for the Twin Princes fight
  • Give Coals to Andre
  • Upgrade Estus Flask
  • Send Greirat away to scavenge
  • Upgrade Bonfire intensity

Lothric Castle - Grand Archives

  • Grab Greirat's Ashes (Located on a lower roof of the Grand Archives, after you drop down from the upper roof)
  • Grab Undead Bone Shard (On a corpse sitting in a chair at the top of Grand Archives after venturing over the rooftops)
  • Grab Estus Shard (Located on the roof of the Grand Archives near the three Gertrude's Winged Knights)
  • Kill the Twin Princes (You can summon Orbeck for this fight if you purchased all his spells and spoke to him about leaving)
  • OPTIONAL Grab Orbeck's Ashes (From his corpse in the Grand Archives, just past the first elevator shortcut) if you caused him to leave Firelink Shrine

Firelink Shrine pit stop 6

  • DO NOT give Orbeck's Ashes to the Shrine Handmaid (Give them to Yuria instead)
  • OPTIONAL Kill Orbeck if you don't already have his Ashes (But be sure to buy any spells from him that you might still need because they'll get lost)
  • Give Greirat's Ashes to Shrine Handmaid
  • Upgrade Estus Flask & Bonfire intensity for the last time

Irithyll of the Boreal Valley - Anor Londo

  • Get Sword of Avowal (Speak to the Pilgrim Assassin)
  • Perform marriage ceremony with Anri's corpse

Final things to do in Firelink Shrine

  • Kill Unbreakable Patches (If it's not for his annoying location in Firelink Shrine, it's for revenge or because he's ripping us off)
  • Kill Yuria for her Ashes (And drops)
  • Give both Ashes to Shrine Handmaid

You've made it! Whish to start over? Click the reset run button
